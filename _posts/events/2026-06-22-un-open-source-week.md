## UN Open Source Week 2026

* **What:** A global convening at the United Nations bringing together open source
  maintainers, technologists, and policymakers — [UN Open Source Week](https://www.unopensource.org/)
* **When:** June 22–26, 2026
* **Where:** United Nations Headquarters, New York, NY
* **Agenda:** [View the online agenda](https://www.unopensource.org/agenda)

pyOpenSci Executive Director Leah Wasser returns to UN Open Source Week as a
sponsored maintainer, supported by the
[Sovereign Tech Agency](https://www.sovereign.tech/). This year she will
facilitate a session on open source and AI tools — and participate across a full
week of programming spanning maintainer sustainability, AI, digital public
infrastructure, and open source policy.

Last year, Leah participated in and facilitated sessions at the UN Tech Over
Hackathon — part of a growing relationship between pyOpenSci and the broader
open source policy and sustainability community.

## Week at a Glance

**Monday, June 22 — Maintain-a-thon**
Leah participates as a sponsored maintainer in a hands-on day dedicated to
open source sustainability and maintainer support.

**Tuesday, June 23 — Open Source × AI Day**
A full day exploring the intersection of open source and AI, beginning with a
breakfast reception hosted by the Sovereign Tech Agency featuring open source
leaders and policymakers. Leah facilitates a session on open source and AI
tools.

**Wednesday, June 24 — Digital Public Infrastructure Day**
Programming focused on open source as critical digital public infrastructure
globally.
